Chowmein

Homepage Description

Chowmein is a popular dish in Nepal.It is made up of flour from Rice. It looks like Noodles but it doesn't have any soup.

It comes in various color. Color depends on the cooking technique and material used. It also contains veggies of various types accorfing to the season.

Ingredients

  • Chowmein pack
  • Chilli
  • Salt
  • Onion
  • Cabbage or Capsicum

How to prepare Chowmein?

  1. Warm the water and make it lukewarm
  2. Rinse the Chowmein in it and drain the water when Chowmein is soft
  3. Heat the pan and put some oil
  4. After the oil is heated, cook ingredients one by one till CHowmein turn brown
