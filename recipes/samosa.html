Samosa

Description

Samosa is another popular dish in Nepal. It is used in breakfast. It is prepared by using flour, boiled potato, some cumin and turmeric.

Ingredients Required

  • Flour
  • Cumin seeds
  • BOiled potato
  1. Boil and mash the potato.
  2. Take a wet flour and give it a shape of funnel some sharp edges
  3. Fill in the funnel wih ingredients above
  4. Roast the samosa in oil by dipping it till it turns red
